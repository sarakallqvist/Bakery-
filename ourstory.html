Our Story

The Sweetest of Stories

Regina has been immersed in the world of bread and bready products since the tender age of seven, back when her great grand-nana would regularly take a pinch of dough from last week’s loaf to make the coming week’s sourdough. She ventured off to school back in the ‘80s to become a pastry chef, and opened Regina bakery in 1991, always remembering her ‘nana’s diligence and dedication to breadmaking.

As the years have gone by, Regina and the expert bakers at both her downtown and marina locations have trained almost one hundred bakers, giving them apprenticeships with a solid foundation in bread-baking, pastry work and cake decorating. Most importantly, these apprenticeships have passed on a time-honored tradition,a sense of professionalism and a drive to always learn and experiment with new creations. Regina’s apprentices are the best in town – and beyond! Our bakers have gone on to work in seven different countries, catering for high level events as well as intimate family gatherings. Our graduates have made cakes for their parents as well as important heads of state – even religious figures! It seems like everybody around the world enjoys a good slice of cake.

Locally, Regina Bakery has catered over seven thousand events in its illustrious history, bringing refinement, joy and deliciousness to untold numbers of happy bellies. We thank our clients for sticking with us throughout the years.

Thank you!
